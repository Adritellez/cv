About me!

Graphic Designer graduated from CUN University in Bogotá, Colombia, in 2009 but active in the design business since 2008.
I was born in Bogotá and live in Buenos Aires since 2010, where I took several design and illustration courses.

During some years I worked in digital print and offset art, proffesional printer operations, as well as print and cut plotter, photographic retouch and layouting. I also worked in design for the textile industry, creating all kinds of branding and decorative pieces for clothes, wearables and packaging. In the last couple of years I also worked in design for social media.

Nowadays I'm focused in the digital area, doing user experience and interface design for websites and mobile apps.
